你的研究方向(VR介绍)

My favourite research direction is virtual reality. The virtue reality tech provide a new way to interact with digital world. The applications of VR technology are very extensive, not limited to games and entertainment, but also involving education and training, tourism and sightseeing, healthcare, art, culture, and many other fields. With the increasing maturity of technology, the VR content market is also constantly enriching. What we should know is that when people use the VR equipments,their immersion goes first.
